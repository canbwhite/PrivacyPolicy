Privacy Policy

Jumper Cube

This privacy policy explains how the Jumper Cube application handles user information.

Information Collected

Jumper Cube does not collect or request any personal information from users (such as name, email, phone number, or location).

Advertising

The application may display ads through third-party providers (e.g., Google AdMob). These providers may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver more relevant ads. This data is not collected or stored by the application developer.

Data Sharing

Since Jumper Cube does not collect personal data, no information is shared with third parties. Any data collected by advertising providers is subject to their own privacy policies.

Security

The application does not store or process personal information, so no user data is retained.

Advertising ID

The application may use the Google Advertising ID to deliver personalized or non-personalized ads through AdMob. This ID is managed by Google and does not personally identify the user. Users can reset or disable the Advertising ID in their device settings.
